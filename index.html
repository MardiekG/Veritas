About Me
My name is Mardiek, and my motivation for creating this project began when I watched The 21. It tells the true story of 21 men — most from Egypt and one from Somalia — who were killed by ISIS because they refused to deny their faith in Jesus Christ.

Watching their final moments didn't just inform me — it confronted me. I saw ordinary workers who chose faith over fear, truth over survival. That realization changed how I look at belief. Faith is not just something comforting or cultural; it can demand everything. It can cost a life. And yet, for them, Christ was worth more than breath.

I created this app as a response to that conviction. I wanted a place that remembers their witness and invites people to reflect on what faith really means when it is tested. This project is not just information — it is testimony.

This project exists to remember them, to honor their sacrifice, and to encourage others to stand firm in what they believe.
